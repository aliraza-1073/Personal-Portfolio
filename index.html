About

A quiet, research-first
approach to design.

I follow a user-centered, iterative process — starting with research and ideation, then translating findings into wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes that hold up under real use.

Over the last decade I've worked inside product teams and as an independent partner, shipping interfaces for fintech, health and developer-tooling companies. I care about the fifteen small decisions behind every screen as much as the big idea.
